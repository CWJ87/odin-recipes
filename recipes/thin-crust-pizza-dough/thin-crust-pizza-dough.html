Thin-Crust Pizza Dough

A quick and easy thin-crust pizza dough recipe, ready to bake in just 15 minutes, promises deliciously crisp results.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • ¼ teaspoon white sugar
  • ¾ cup lukewarm water
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water that's 110 degrees F (44 degrees C) in a small bowl. Let stand until yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam, about 5 to 8 minutes.
  2. Combine 1 ¾ cups flour and salt in a large bowl. Pour in yeast mixture; mix well until dough comes together.
  3. Transfer dough to a floured surface and knead until smooth, about 2 minutes. Add remaining ¼ cup flour if dough is too sticky.
  4. Roll dough into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to a greased pizza pan.
